Before You Begin
  • The exam is structured to be a learning experience, to ensure all are aligned and able to apply the recent updates in General Policies and Procedures.
  • Estimated time to complete can be 20–45 minutes.
  • There are 30 questions. A perfect score (100%) is required to receive your completion certificate. You may retake as many times as needed.
  • Results with missed items are shown immediately after exam submission. You may take note of the missed items so you can get it right on the next try.
  • Allowed: Open Manuals and digital references.
  • NOT allowed: Group or joint activity  |  Exam by proxy (like the FBMO).
